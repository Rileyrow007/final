          Launched in the fall of 2020 and run by Managing Director, Riley Rowland. Rowland's Dinner is a multi faceted agency that is customer centric, intentional about ensuring customer gratification and providing top notch service delivery. Our entertainment prowess is world class and highly refined, we assist our clients with precise and novel methods as regards to entertainment, memories and euphoria. At the agency, we are passionate about satisfaction, we execute themes based on the in-depth atmosphere of the community in the moment, finding a center emotion that the masses can relate to.

Rowland's Dinner is structured around being an entertainment agency yet at the same time pioritized at being a brand and with customer consulatation being a vital part of this strategy, we are able to tune every event according to the wants of the Guests. With each interaction and feedback, we build ourselves to be more than an agency, we become a sensation; indescribable and irreplaceable.


Mr. Rowland

MANAGING DIRECTOR
Born on the 19th of May, 1990 in a little town within the buckeye state USA, Riley Rowland has always been concerned with creating and giving sensations beyond the scope of expectations. He started out as a chef, making rounds while attending college, where he discovered his talent for food. After college and with a Bachelor's degree in the collection, Riley set out to be an event planner, creating moments and bringing smiles to people's hearts. But this was not enough, he was still bound by a particular set of norm themes; weddings, birthdays and the rest. This hurdle led to the birth of Rowland's Dinner, Mr. Rowland wanted no boundaries, he wanted to create with an empty canvas.
