Payments

Why are tickets and merchandise not listed on the site?

+

Rowland's Dinner is not a commerce business and our services are limited to event management and consultation. Merchandise can be gotten via our Amazon link and windows for ticket purchase will be provided once preparations for an event is in session.

Why can't I purchase a ticket?

+

Please confirm the requirements of the event first, as events differ in rules, location, theme, etc. Do this before trying another method of payment. Contact our customer care if the issue persists.

Is Rowland's Dinner a brand or an event?

+

We are a business committed to hosting spectacular events, all bearing a trademark of perfection.

What if my payment does not reflect?

+

After making payments, a confirmation message will be sent to the corresponding invite e-mail address. If you don't get this message please wait; if after 48 hours, please mail our customer care line for immediate payment confirmation.

How many events are hosted annually?

+

At the moment, Rowland's Dinner hosts on average seven (7) events yearly. This number is set to rise to twelve (12).

Why the third party payment option?

+

In an attempt to add an extra layer of personality in the customer - company relatonship; being that the Agency is quite young, we have agreed to incoorporate the Agent payment option, where funds for tickets and services can deposited into a personal account with a face and ID to go by; should any customer become weary of fraudulent mishap.
